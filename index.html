About

Short version of how I ended up here.

I’m a final-year Industrial Engineering student from Mexicali, currently working as a remote logistics analyst and building data-heavy projects around supply chains and economics.

I grew up as the younger brother following a very strong act – same school, same sports, same cities. For a long time I was “good enough” at everything, but never the one people pointed to. Over the last couple of years, that changed: I started doing real work in logistics, building systems around data instead of just using tools, and aiming for places that used to feel out of reach.

This site is mostly a record of that shift: the things I build, the questions I’m chasing, and what I’m currently learning.
